## About ICCoSD-26

The International Conference on Communication and Smart Devices (ICCoSD) is a premier platform for researchers, industry experts, and academics to discuss innovations in communication systems and smart device technologies. This biennial conference brings together leading minds to share their latest research and insights.

Organized by the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering at Birla Institute of Technology Mesra, this year's conference features cutting-edge research papers, interactive keynote sessions, and networking opportunities.
